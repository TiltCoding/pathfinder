# Rubric: exploration quality (`exploration.md`)

- **Relevant files found** — the key files for the task are listed with clickable `path:line` refs.
- **Entry points & flow** — how control/data reaches the area is described.
- **Reuse identified** — existing utilities/patterns to build on are called out.
- **Constraints & risks** — invariants and failure modes are surfaced.
- **Commands** — test/build commands for the area are captured.
- **Open questions** — genuine decisions are raised (not trivial ones with obvious defaults).
- **Knowledge reuse** — if `docs/knowledge/` existed, the explorer reused it instead of re-deriving.

Good exploration points precisely at the code; it doesn't summarize vaguely or dump whole files.
